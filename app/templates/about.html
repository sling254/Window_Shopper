WHY CHOOSE US ?

Business owners have various options such as traditional classified platforms or simply making the use of social media platforms such as Facebook & Instagram.However Windowshopper gives them the power to manage their online inventory just as they would in their physical store.

With the Windowshopper app, business owners can upload as much inventory as they want, specify how many of each is in stock, adjust prices as they wish, change photos and even add product variations. All under one application.

Furthermore, Windowshopper assures you that with every sale, your product will be picked up & delivered within 24hrs, and your payment will be sent directly to your MPesa account immediately the customer accepts the delivery.
